HIKMAH is a comprehensive Islamic Banking Solution developed by ERA based on Core Principals of Sharia. It offers the industry’s widest range of software solutions to Islamic banks addressing all their Business & Operational requirements.

HIKMAH provides Real Time Shariah Banking. Profit Distribution process is driven by a set of parameters that allow the users to group profit received from investment such as (Bai-Murabaha, HPSM etc.) with payable expense generated from liability business activity such as (Mudaraba accounts). Allow special profit distribution as per agreed ratio. Support both ISR & Weighted Average Method in Profit Calculation.
