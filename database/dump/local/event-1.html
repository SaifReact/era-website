An Agreement Signing Ceremony between ERA-InfoTech Ltd and CR2 Ltd was held to provide sophisticated software services to banks on 20 April 2019 in presence of Mr. A. Rouf Chowdhury, Chairman of ERA-InfoTech Ltd and Bank Asia Ltd in the Capital City. Mr. Fintan Byrne, Chief Executive Officer, CR2 and Md. Serajul Islam, FCMA, CEO of ERA-Infotech Ltd. signed on the agreement on behalf of their respective organizations.

Mr. Nafees Khundker, Director of ERA-InfoTech Ltd, Mr. Enam Chowdhury, Director, Mr. Mohammad Borhanuddin, President & Managing Director (CC), Mr. Sarder Akhter Hamed, Head of Channel Banking, Mr. Hossain Ahmad, CIO, Mr. Md. Saiful Islam, CTO from Bank Asia Ltd, Mr. Andrew Chisembele, SVP, Sales from CR2 and Mr. Tauhidul Hoque, CTO along with other high officials of ERA-Infotech were present in the program.

CR2 Ltd, an Irish company, proudly serves over 100 banks in 60 countries including ANZ & SCB, providing value and making banking simple. They provide innovative omnichannel banking software to banks all around the world, from ATM to mobile and POS to internet, they make self-service banking better.

ERA-InfoTech Ltd, the pioneer in automation of financial inclusion and software development in Bangladesh will market the switching software jointly with CR2 in this region as a technology partner.
