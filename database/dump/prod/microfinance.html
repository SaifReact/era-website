Ektee Bari Ektee Khamar i.e. One House One Farm is a poverty alleviation project of the government of Bangladesh through e-financial inclusion followed by family farming livelihood and income generation of the under privileged and poor of the country. ERA-InfoTech Ltd. has to come up with a solution that can not only monitor the Loan related process but also has the ability to analyze the real time scenario of the project.

ERA developed EBdesh Micro Finance Solution, a full-featured web application powered by a variety of advanced technologies that provides a real time Monitoring with Management dashboard.

ERA has achieved Manthan Award South Asia and Asia Pacific 2013 in Business and Financial Inclusion Category for its EBdesh- Micro Finance Solution.

Features:
  • Bio-metric enrollment
  • Centralized administration
  • Audit trail on all transaction
  • Dash-board driven information
  • Online daily consolidated reports
  • Fully real time web-based solution
  • Highly customized and parameterized
  • Unlimited member of groups and users
  • Quick and easy data backup and restore
  • Each operation is mapped with a unique ID
