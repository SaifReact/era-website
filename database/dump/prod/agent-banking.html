ERA develops Agent Banking Solution to provide banking services to unbanked people, bring mass rural people under Financial Inclusion, involve ICT innovations, capture data through Bio-metric technology and enhance green technology. Agent banking transactions are done on real time basis and integrated with Bank’s Core Banking solution. Customers get instant confirmation of transaction through Mobile SMS and system generated Printed money receipts.
