Advantageous, User-friendly, dynamic, robust, extremely interactive and highly parameterized HR Solution is the dream of any small to large scale organization. ERA’s HR & Payroll Management Solution – “Powerbits” has it all. “Powerbits” is Certified by Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore (IRAS).

Powerbits HR Module supports organization definition, recruitment, visa processing and employment. Employment includes employee profile maintenance, compensation and leave management and end of service benefits processing. Training and appraisals are optional and a detailed growth profile is maintained by the system.

Powerbits Payroll includes time & attendance processing, loans and advances management, incentives administration and annual bonus computations, tax deduction/calculations. Manpower costing with cost center allocations is also included, where necessary.
