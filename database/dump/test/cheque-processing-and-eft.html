ERA-InfoTech Ltd has developed e-MICR solution under the guideline of Central Bank of Bangladesh which is a Centralized Online Automatic Cheque Processing System. It helps banks to meet legal and regulatory requirement, reduces cheque processing costs, improve productivity with automated technical verification, reduce mitigates risk and eliminates delays.

Features:
  • Centralized Online Electronic Fund Transfer System.
  • Data can be storage centralized.
  • Supports various distributed operating system.
  • Oracle database server for both front office as well as back office operation Independent of the back office.
  • Outward clearing likes upload data and image from scanner, data entry by maker, data verifications by checker, and Inward Clearing likes Upload Data and Image From PBM, Collect Data, Branch Wise Distribution, Branch Wise Distribution process are automatically functionalized.
  • Client Communication through mail or SMS.
