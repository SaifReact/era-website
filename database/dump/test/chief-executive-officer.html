MR. MD. SERAJUL ISLAM, FCMA

CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Mr. Md. Serajul islam completed his graduation and post graduation from Dhaka University. After completion of his post-graduation, he completed Professional Course 'Cost and Management Accountants' from ICMAB, he achieved Diploma in Association of Bankers, Bangladesh (DAIBB) in 2004. He completed an international course on Business Innovation by Information Technology (BIIT), from AOTS, Japan and "Advanced Management Executive Program-Education" course from The University of Hong Kong.

Prior to joining ERA-InfoTech Limited, As the Chief Executive Officer Mr. Md. Serajul Islam started his banking career as Financial Analyst in Sonali Bank Limited. Later, he served Bank Asia Limited as the Deputy Head of ICT department as well as Business Analyst.

He is also involved in the largest Poverty Alleviation Project of Bangladesh Government “Ektee Bari Ektee Khamar (EBEK)”. With his leadership skills and sound technical knowledge, the automation of this specialized Micro Finance Management project which brings marginal mass people under the financial inclusion through the optimum utilization of ICT innovation has been successfully completed.
